Do you have Depression?

Take this questionnaire to find out!

Do you worry about things, such as work or school, more days than not?





Do you find it difficult to stop thoughts related to worrying?





Do you often feel restless or on edge even when nothing is happening to cause these feelings?





Is it hard for you to concentrate on specific tasks or do you often notice your mind just "going blank"?





Do you often feel irritable or tense even when nothing is going on which would justify this feeling?





Is it difficult for you to fall asleep due to too many thoughts in your head?





Do you notice your muscles getting tense frequently or feel tension in your lower back, neck, or eyes?





Do you find it difficult to sit still without having to fiddle with something, doodle, or make other repetitive movements?





Have you noticed periods during the day when you have symptoms such as heart palpatations, sweaty plans, or shallow breathing?





Do friends or family members tell you that you are too high strung, worry too much about little things, or need to "chill"?
